Steps to make strawberry smoothie

  1. Place frozen strawberries, orange juice, greek yogurt, kale, bananas, and a blender on the counter
  2. Open the blender and put in 1/2 cup of strawberries and a handful of kale
  3. Cut the banana into small pieces put those pieces into the blender as well
  4. Measure 3/4 cup of greek yogurt and 1/4 cup of orange juice and pour those into the blender
  5. Blend to the desired consistency
  6. Enjoy!
